Introduction

Welcome to my personal website. I was a joint Ph.D. from the South China University of Technology and the University of California, Irvine. Supervised by Sharad Mehrotra and Xiaohui Xie. I am currently an assistant professor at Anhui University (211 Project & Double-first Class University). His interests include complex networks, network embedding, and graph neural networks. He has published papers in AAAI, WWW, DASFAA, Physica A, IEEE Transactions on Network Science and Engineering, IEEE Transactions on Human-Machine Systems, Science China Information Sciences, etc
